Key Insights

📋 Issue 📝 Details ⚡ Action Item
Critical Streaming Reliability Issues
78 reviews
 Users consistently report frequent buffering, connectivity problems, and stream quality degradation across different devices and network conditions, severely impacting viewing experience. Investigate CDN performance and implement adaptive streaming improvements to reduce buffering and connectivity issues
App Crashes and Performance Problems
65 reviews
 The Netflix app frequently crashes during startup, while browsing content, or in the middle of watching shows, forcing users to restart the app repeatedly. Prioritize app stability fixes and conduct comprehensive testing across different device configurations and OS versions
Content Discovery and Search Issues
52 reviews
 Users struggle to find specific content due to poor search functionality and algorithm recommendations that don't match their preferences or viewing history. Enhance search algorithms and recommendation engine to better surface relevant content based on user behavior and preferences
Auto-play and Interface Frustrations
43 reviews
 Intrusive auto-play features, difficulty navigating the interface, and removal of user-friendly features are creating significant user experience friction. Provide granular user controls for auto-play features and redesign navigation to be more intuitive and user-friendly
